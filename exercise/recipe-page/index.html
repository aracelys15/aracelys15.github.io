NASHVILLE HOT CHICKEN SANDWICHES

  • PREP TIME: 30 MINUTES
  • COOK TIME: 1 HOUR
  • TOTAL TIME: 1 HOUR 30 MINUTES
  • YIELDS: 4 TO 6 SANDWICHES
  • Jump to recipe

    • The hot chicken craze is taking over the food scene like wildfire but let me tell you, the best Nashville chicken you can get is the one you make at home and even better! From the crispy flakey fried chicken to the creamy coleslaw and tangy sauce, this sandwich is built to perfection in the flavor AND texture department.     INGREDIENTS

    CHICKEN

    • 2 large chicken breasts, about 1.5 pounds
    • peanut or vegetable oil, for frying

    DRY

    • 1 cup all purpose flour
    • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
    • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
    • 2 tablespoons chili powder
    • 2 teaspoons salt
    • 2 teaspoons dry mustard powder
    • 1 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder

    WET

    • 3/4 cup buttermilk
    • 1 large egg
    • 2 tablespoons vinegar-based hot sauce
    • 1/4 cup pickle juice
    • 1 teaspoon baking powder

    SPICY OIL

    • 1 tablespoon light brown sugar
    • 2 1/2 tablespoons cayenne powder, add more to taste
    • 2 tablespoons chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon dry mustard powder
    • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
    • 1 teaspoon salt, more to taste
    • 3/4 cup frying oil

    COLESLAW

    • 3 cups coleslaw blend or 2 cups shredded green cabbage, 1/2 cup purple cabbage, 1/2 cup shredded carrots
    • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
    • 1/2 to 1 tablespoon yellow mustard, adjust to taste
    • salt, to taste
    • black pepper, to taste
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice or white vinegar
    • 1/2 to 1 tablespoon granulated white sugar

    SAUCE

    • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
    • 1/4 cup ketchup
    • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce
    • 1 teaspoon black pepper
    • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

    OTHER

    • 4 brioche buns
    • unsalted butter, for toasting buns
    • pickles
